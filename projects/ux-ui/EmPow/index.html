Project Summary

EmPow was created to empower the youth in finding their confidence to start building their careers through learning about financial literacy and finding opportunities. Originally we intended to compete in the Infy App Design Challenge but did not reach semi-finals. However, we did win Overall Excellence in the Emoti-Con 2025 competition.

My Role

I served as the Group Leader & Communications, responsible for communication between my team and our mentors, overseeing progress among my teammates, assisting in designing pages, and offering assistance throughout the project development process.

Context & Challenge

The goal was to address financial literacy and employment for teens and younger adults. The youth are the future and are what continues after us. If we are able to offer guidance and give them a better start, we will advance further and eventually do greater things.
