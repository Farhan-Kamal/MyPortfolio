Project Overview

Subartic Island is a unique Club Penguin private server that reimagines the classic game by allowing players to play as polar bears instead of the traditional penguins. This creative twist on the beloved game required a complete visual rebrand, including logo design, emoji creation, and overall brand identity development.

My Role

I was responsible for the complete visual design of Subartic Island, including:

  • Logo Design - Creating a distinctive brand mark that represents the polar bear theme
  • Emoji Design - Designing custom emojis for the Discord server and in-game communication
  • Brand Identity - Developing the overall visual language and style guide
  • Marketing Assets - Creating promotional graphics and social media content

Design Process

The design process involved several key phases:

  1. Research & Discovery - Studying polar bear characteristics and Club Penguin's visual style
  2. Concept Development - Creating initial sketches and mood boards
  3. Logo Iteration - Refining the logo through multiple design iterations
  4. Emoji Creation - Designing a comprehensive set of custom emojis
  5. Brand Guidelines - Establishing consistent visual standards
